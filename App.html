THE O APP - invisible, yet always there for you

  • Highly intuitive with minimal involvement from you.
  • Uniquely proactive and immediately responsive.
  • Intelligently adapts to your behavior.

GET STARTED IN SECONDS

THE O automatically recognizes the color of your new device.

Attach your new O to a belonging. Select an icon and give it a name to make it easy to identify.

If you mark your belonging as ‘essential’ we will make sure you remember it wherever you go.

LOSE LESS, FORGET LESS, WORRY LESS

THE O is an app that silently works in the background. It’s here to help you when you need it and give you peace of mind.

  • Immediate alert in the very moment you are about to lose something; camera, umbrella, jacket.
  • Get a reminder when you leave home without your wallet, medicine or other essentials.
  • The app checks for all your things when you leave the office, a restaurant or a friends place.

THE O ALWAYS KNOWS WHERE YOUR BELONGINGS ARE

If you open the app you can see where everything is.

  • “Safe” is what you left at home.
  • “With me” are all the items that you currently have on you.
  • “Temp. safe” are the things you have, say, in a coat check, or at a friend’s party. We’ll make sure you don’t leave without them.

DON’T WORRY, WE HAVE YOUR BACK

THE O is proactive and tells you immediately if you are about to leave something behind.

Should you ever miss one of our notifications, we still make sure you know when and where you left your belonging so you can retrieve it.
